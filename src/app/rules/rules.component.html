What’s a Skyrim Roulette?

Skyrim Roulette is a way to purposefully handicap yourself during your next playthrough of Skyrim. By deliberatly avoiding to fall into your favorite playstyle, a Roulette will give you a set of randomly selected restrictions, that will force the player to think outside of their comfort zone and look for solutions in ways they hadn’t thought of before. It then follows that the builds that you’ll be playing in a Roulette run are considerably less powerful than free-range builds. Skyrim is easy when you glitch grind your way to level 100 in Enchanting, Smithing, Alchemy and Sneak, but what if the Roulette prevents you from doing this? Will you know where to look for alternatives?

How does it work?

Joe’s Skyrim Roulette (JSR) will randomly generate the following on each roll:

  • A race
  • 3 Major Skills
  • 3 Minor Skills
  • Zero or more handicaps
  • A quest

The goal of the game is to start a new Skyrim playthrough from scratch and successfully complete the quest you’ve been assigned. As with normal Skyrim, there’s no restriction to what you can do in order to achieve this goal except:

  • Your hero has to be of the race assigned by the Roulette. You’re free to choose the sex, as well as the physical appearance.
  • Until you reach level 20, you can only spend perk points on your Major Skills.
  • After you reach level 20, you can also spend perk points on your Minor Skills.
  • If you’ve been assigned one or more handicap, check the following table to see what restrictions they add.

Note about the difficulty level

It is tempting to impose to play on Legendary level, in order to make this Roulette really challenging. However, given that some of the builds generated are fairly weak, I fear it could turn off new players. So I’d rather take the same approach as the Skyrim designers and let the player decide for themselves what they want to play. I personally play most of my playthroughs on Master difficulty, it’s difficult enough that you cannot simply rush into fights and dungeons, yet not too difficult so you don’t need to spend an eternity doing trade and business.
