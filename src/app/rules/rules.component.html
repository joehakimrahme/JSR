Rules

  • You get assigned a race, 3 Major Skills, 3 Minor Skills, a Quest, zero or more handicaps
  • The game ends when you finish the assigned quest's mainline. You're free to do whatever before then.
  • Until you reach lvl20, you can only spend perk points on your Major Skill trees. After you reach 20, you can also spend perk points on Minor Skill trees.

Handicaps

HandicapExplanation
InsomniacYou cannot sleep
StonedYou cannot get any stone signs
Pious MortalYou don't worship daedra and won't seek any daedric artifact
Disgrace to your RaceYou cannot use any active racial ability. The passive ones like resistance to magic/poison/element are still usable
Body OdourYou're so repulsive you cannot have a follower
DragonfoolYou're not the Dragonborn. Never kill the first dragon. Never learn to shout.
AgoraphobicYou cannot do any trading in shops within cities.
TravelerYou refuse to fast travel. Horse travel and carriages are still okay
PermadeathYou die once, you delete the character and start again
