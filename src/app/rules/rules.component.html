Rules

  • You get assigned a race, 3 Major Skills, 3 Minor Skills, a Quest, zero or more handicaps
  • The game ends when you finish the assigned quest's mainline. You're free to do whatever before then.
  • Until you reach lvl20, you can only spend perk points on your Major Skill trees. After you reach 20, you can also spend perk points on Minor Skill trees.

Handicaps

HandicapExplanation
InsomniacThe voices in your head prevent you from falling asleep. The loud screams of your own anxiety keep you up and dreading the night. You cannot sleep in-game.
StonedYears of substance abuse have made your body insensitive to natural wonders. You remain completely apathetic around Standing Stones and refuse to accept the sign of their blessing.
Pious MortalYears of prayers have strenghtened your faith in the Divines. You reject the daedras and their cult, you refuse to pursue any daedric artifact
Disgrace to your RaceYour own culture makes no sense to you, you have no natural affinity to your own kin. You cannot use any active racial power you may have
Body OdourBad breath, poor hygiene, and horrible manners. Nobody appreciates your companionship. You cannot pick up a follower.
DragonfoolYou're not the Dragonborn. Never kill the first dragon. Never learn to shout. You're just a citizen of Skyrim like everybody else.
AgoraphobicYou hate crowds and they hate you. You avoid towns when you can. You cannot do any trading in shops within cities.
TravelerYou refuse to fast travel. Horse travel and carriages are still okay
PermadeathYou die once, you delete the character and start again.
